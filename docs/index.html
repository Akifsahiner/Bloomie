Privacy Policy - Bloomie

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

Bloomie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Bloomie (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username (when you create an account)
  • Profile Data: Names and details of your plants, pets, or babies that you track in the App
  • Activity Logs: Text entries, voice recordings, and photos you upload to track health and care activities
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features you use, timestamps
  • Technical Data: App version, crash reports, performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the App's core functionality (health tracking, reminders, AI chat)
  • Generate personalized insights and weekly reports
  • Process AI-powered health analysis of photos
  • Send you notifications and reminders for care activities
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Ensure App security and prevent fraud

Data Storage and Processing

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a cloud database service. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

AI Processing

  • OpenAI GPT-4o: We use OpenAI's API (via secure Supabase Edge Functions) to:
    • Process chat conversations
    • Analyze journal entries
    • Generate health insights
    • Parse voice commands
  • Perplexity AI: We use Perplexity's API (via secure Supabase Edge Functions) for:
    • Web-grounded search queries
    • Real-time information retrieval
    • Critical health-related queries

Important: Your data is sent to these AI services only through secure server-side functions. API keys are never exposed in the App.

Permissions We Request

Camera

  • Purpose: To take photos for health analysis
  • Usage: Photos are analyzed by AI to assess health conditions

Photo Library

  • Purpose: To access photos you want to analyze
  • Usage: Selected photos are uploaded for AI health analysis

Microphone

  • Purpose: For voice mode to log activities hands-free
  • Usage: Voice recordings are transcribed and processed by AI

Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you reminders for care activities
  • Usage: Local and push notifications for scheduled tasks

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  1. AI Service Providers: With OpenAI and Perplexity (via secure server functions) to provide AI features
  2. Service Providers: With Supabase for data storage and hosting
  3. Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Secure API key management (server-side only)
  • Regular security audits

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Opt-out: Disable certain features or delete your account

To exercise these rights, contact us at: akifsahinerr@gmail.com

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently delete all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Bloomie account and all associated data at any time:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open Bloomie app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to akifsahinerr@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account information (email, name)
  • All your nurtures (plants, pets, babies)
  • All log entries and activity history
  • All photos you uploaded
  • All reminders and settings
  • AI chat history

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

Bloomie is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
